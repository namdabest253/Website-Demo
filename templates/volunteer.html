One-on-One Mentorship

As a mentor, you will provide 2-3 students for our 10-week program. You'll provide career guidance, review resumes, conduct mock interviews, and help students navigate their professional journey.

  • Time Commitment: 1 hour per week
  • Duration: 4 months
  • Format: Virtual meetings
  • Experience: 3+ years coaching/managing others
