One-on-One Mentorship

As a mentor, you'll be paired with 2-3 students for our 10-week program. You'll provide career guidance, review resumes, conduct mock interviews, and help students navigate their professional journey.

  • Time Commitment: 2-3 hours per week
  • Duration: 10-week program cycles
  • Format: Virtual meetings
  • Experience: 3+ years in your field

  • Time Commitment: 1-2 hours per session
  • Frequency: 2-4 sessions per program cycle
  • Group Size: 8-12 students
  • Expertise: Leadership or training experience preferred
