Our Story

Empowering first-generation and under-resourced college students to build meaningful careers through supportive professional development and community.

Our Mission

Driving Forward empowers first-generation and under-resourced college students to successfully transition from college to a meaningful career through a supportive professional development, mentorship, and experiential learning community. Every college student in America should have an equal opportunity to achieve their career objectives and create a better future for themselves and their communities.

About Driving Forward

Since its inception in 2020, Driving Forward has been a catalyst for success for over 250 college students exploring career paths.

Our Vision

We offer unique opportunities to develop essential skills, deliver business impact, and build invaluable social capital. Our program accelerates the development of first-generation students, removing barriers and friction in career exploration.

Our Impact

Through comprehensive professional development, mentorship, and real-world experience, we've helped hundreds of students launch successful careers and build the confidence needed to thrive in their chosen fields.

Our Community

We believe in the power of community and connection. Our network of mentors, volunteers, and alumni creates lasting relationships that extend far beyond the program duration, providing ongoing support and opportunities.
