Professional Mentorship

Driving Forward unlocks social capital by connecting our interns with mentors. The mentors provide one-on-one coaching, soft skills development, career fair preparation, and access to networks. The mentorship role accelerates career discovery, removing a barrier for first generation and under-resourced college students.
