Driving Forward Vision

Every college student in America should have an equal opportunity to achieve their career objectives and create a better future for themselves and their communities.  

Driving Forward Mission

Driving Forward empowers first-generation and under-resourced college students to successfully transition from college to a meaningful career through a supportive professional development, mentorship, and experiential learning community.
